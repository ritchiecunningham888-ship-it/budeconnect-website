Extra charges

Night, holiday, and special booking rates

Daytime fares

Daytime fares apply between 6:00 am and 10:00 pm.

Night rate

Pickups between 10:00 pm and 6:00 am may be charged at 15% above the standard daytime fare.

Bank holidays and peak dates

Bank holiday bookings may be charged at 20% above the daytime fare. Christmas Day, Boxing Day, and New Year’s Day bookings may be charged at 30% above the daytime fare.

What affects price?

How fares are worked out

Distance and destination

Longer journeys naturally cost more, and airport or station transfers are priced according to mileage, route planning, and journey time.

Time and waiting

Early-morning pickups, late-night travel, return journeys, or bookings that involve waiting time may be priced differently from standard daytime trips.

Special requirements

Extra stops, large luggage loads, child seats, accessibility needs, or bespoke arrangements can all affect the final confirmed fare.

Pricing notes

Before you book

Guide prices only

Prices on this page are example daytime fares and may change. Final fares are always confirmed at the time of booking.

Minimum local fare

Minimum local fare is £10 in daytime hours and £12 for night bookings.

Pre-booked service

BudeConnect operates on an advanced-booking basis. Same-day availability may be limited, especially for airport and long-distance trips.
